Bihar's premier ecosystem for Neurological excellence and advanced physical rehabilitation.

Prism Healthcare and Rehabilitation Center represents a paradigm shift in the landscape of physical therapy. We are an integrated clinical ecosystem dedicated to bridging the critical gap between acute medical discharge and a patient's full return to functional independence.

Our practice is built on the foundation of Evidence-Based Clinical Reasoning. Unlike traditional physiotherapy, we utilize a specialized "Movement Science" approach, integrating advanced protocols like NDT (Neuro-Developmental Treatment), PNF, and high-velocity manual manipulation to treat complex neurological and orthopedic conditions.

At Prism, our mission is to provide metropolitan-standard care locally. By combining compassionate patient-centric care with cutting-edge therapeutic modalities, we ensure that every recovery blueprint is as unique as the patient we serve.

Our  Aim

Our aim is to create a pain free Bihar by becoming a trusted center of clinical excellence where every patient, regardless of how serious their condition may be, can access high quality rehabilitation. We strive to deliver care that matches the standards of major cities, while helping individuals recover effectively, restore function, and return to an independent and active life.

Our   Mission

Our mission is to provide research driven and patient centered physiotherapy through compassionate care and advanced global treatment approaches such as Neuro Developmental Treatment, Proprioceptive Neuromuscular Facilitation, and specialized manual therapy techniques. We are committed to delivering effective, evidence based care that reduces pain, restores function, and improves overall quality of life for every patient.

The Director

Dr.   Ravi   Kumar
MPT (Neurology), MIAP, CONT, CMTP

Dr. Ravi Kumar is an expert physiotherapist known for his strong clinical expertise in evidence based practice and advanced manual therapy, with hands on experience in managing a wide range of musculoskeletal, Orthopedic and neurological conditions.

His specializes in advanced treatment approaches including dry needling, cupping therapy, and comprehensive exercise based rehabilitation, focusing on delivering precise, result oriented care.

Formerly associated with Yatharth Hospital Greater Noida, Park Hospital Gurugram, and Advanced Neuro Hospital Patna, and with experience across multiple clinics, his clinical practice is rooted in evidence based physiotherapy with a strong emphasis on manual therapy and individualized care. He has managed a wide range of cases including stroke, paralysis, and chronic musculoskeletal and spinal conditions, with a clear focus on functional recovery and restoring patient independence.

He has contributed to multiple research publications covering topics such as nomophobia among Indian students, text neck syndrome, work related stress, and anxiety during the COVID 19 pandemic, reflecting his interest in both physical and mental health domains.
